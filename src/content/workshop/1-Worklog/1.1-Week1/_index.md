## WEEK 1 WORKLOG (17/04/2026 – 24/04/2026)

First week at AWS. The onboarding is heavier than I expected, and I mean that in a good way.

I went through the First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ) onboarding start to finish. Created my AWS account, spent a slow afternoon just clicking around the Management Console so I stopped feeling lost in it, and read carefully about Regions and Availability Zones before picking anything. The region we mostly work out of is `ap-southeast-1`, which makes sense sitting in Vietnam.

Set up AWS Budgets with a small monthly cap almost immediately. The horror stories about accidental bills are enough motivation. Also read through the AWS Support tiers so I understand what my mentor is actually paying for when he says "raise a case".

The first hands-on labs were mostly there to make the concepts stick instead of floating around as slides. Nothing scary yet, but I can already feel the difference between reading a service name and having actually used it once.

End of the week I feel like I know less than when I started, which apparently is a normal AWS feeling. Next week is IAM plus a first look at VPC and EC2.
